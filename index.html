We are provider of high quality and aesthetically pleasing resin-based handcrafted products.

Resinance was coined by combining the phrases Resin and Renaissance. The concept behind the brand is that resin is the primary material used to create the finished products, and Renaissance refers to arts, which is the source of inspiration for generating ideas for producing visually impressive products.

Vision: To be the leading provider of high quality and aesthetically pleasing resin-based handcrafted products, as well as a major contributor to the growth of the Philippine arts and crafts industry.

Mission: To create art products that are infused with a spirit of enthusiasm and to show them in a context that values each craft's story.

Resinance is a collection of resin-based arts and crafts products. We offer high-quality, handcrafted products at a reasonable price. All of our products are ideal for gifts for any occasion, and you can personalize them in any way you desire, from color to design to size.
